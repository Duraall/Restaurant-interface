About Catering

Tradition since 1885

Le Catering was founded by Mighty McLea Smith to honor time-honored traditions. Le Catering specializes in the delectable: whipped dill, fresh herb spreads, compound butters, and custom-curated dinner shows. Each bite is a celebration of food and tradition that stretches back to generations. We collaborate with local and sustainable farms to bring the freshest ingredients and highest quality to your table. Whether it's an intimate gathering or a large event, our team is ready to cater to all your needs.

We pride ourselves on creating experiences that bring people together. A meal isn't just a meal; it’s an experience that connects hearts and minds.
